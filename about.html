About Us

Our Company

GMAT Company Limited is one of the main leading companies in packaging solutions of woven and non-woven bags in Kenya and East Africa. The company delivers customized, high quality print solutions for flexible packaging options of both laminated and non-laminated polypropylene bags and jute bags.

Vision: To be the most trusted and innovative packaging partner in Africa.
